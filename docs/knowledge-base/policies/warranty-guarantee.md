### Freshness Guarantee

- **Storage**: Proper storage instructions included

### Taste Guarantee
- **Not satisfied?** Contact us within 14 days

### Defect Coverage
**Covered Issues:**
- Stale or off-flavors
- Incorrect roast level
- Foreign objects in coffee

- Incorrect product shipped

**Not Covered:**

- Brewing method incompatibility
- Storage after opening (beyond 30 days)
