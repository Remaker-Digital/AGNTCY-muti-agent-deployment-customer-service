# Return & Refund Policy

## Overview
We want you to love your coffee! If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a comprehensive return and refund policy designed to ensure your satisfaction.

## Auto-Approval Refunds
**Eligible for Automatic Processing:**
- Orders under $50
- Requested within 30 days of delivery
- Original packaging required
- No questions asked

**Processing Time:** 3-5 business days to original payment method

## Standard Return Process
**For orders over $50 or outside 30-day window:**
- Contact customer service for review
- May require return of product
- Refund amount may vary based on condition

## Product Defects
**Quality Issues:**
- Report within 14 days of delivery
- Always escalated to quality team
- Full refund or replacement guaranteed
- No return shipping required for defective items

## Subscription Refunds
- Current shipment: Follow standard policy above
- Future shipments: Cancel anytime, no refund needed
- Prepaid subscriptions: Prorated refund available

## Non-Refundable Items
- Gift cards
- Digital products (brewing guides, courses)
- Custom blends or personalized items

## How to Request a Refund
1. **Email**: support@[company].com
2. **Chat**: Available 9 AM - 6 PM EST
3. **Phone**: 1-800-COFFEE-1

**Required Information:**
- Order number
- Reason for return
- Photos (if defective)

## Processing Timeline
- **Auto-approved**: 3-5 business days
- **Manual review**: 5-7 business days
- **Defective items**: 1-2 business days (priority)

## Shipping Costs
- **Our error**: We pay return shipping
- **Change of mind**: Customer pays return shipping
- **Defective items**: No return required

## International Orders
- Subject to customs and duties
- Return shipping costs apply
- Processing may take 10-14 business days
