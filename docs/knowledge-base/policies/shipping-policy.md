# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options & Timeframes

### Standard Shipping (Free on orders $35+)
- **Delivery Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Carrier**: USPS Ground
- **Cost**: $4.95 (free on orders $35+)
- **Tracking**: Provided via email

### Expedited Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 2-3 business days
- **Carrier**: USPS Priority Mail
- **Cost**: $8.95
- **Tracking**: Real-time updates

### Express Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 1-2 business days
- **Carrier**: USPS Priority Express
- **Cost**: $24.95
- **Tracking**: Real-time updates + signature required

## Processing Time
- **Standard Orders**: 1-2 business days
- **Subscription Orders**: Same day processing
- **Custom Blends**: 2-3 business days
- **Gift Orders**: 1-2 business days

## Shipping Locations

### Domestic (United States)
- All 50 states supported
- Alaska & Hawaii: Additional 2-3 days
- PO Boxes accepted for standard shipping only

### International
- Canada: 7-14 business days ($15.95)
- Europe: 10-21 business days ($25.95)
- Other countries: Contact for quote

## Address Changes
**Before Shipment:**
- Contact us immediately
- Changes processed within 2 hours during business hours
- No additional fees

**After Shipment:**
- Contact carrier directly
- May incur additional fees
- Delivery delays possible

## Packaging
- **Freshness**: Nitrogen-flushed bags
- **Protection**: Padded mailers or boxes
- **Sustainability**: Recyclable materials
- **Gift Options**: Available at checkout

## Delivery Issues

### Missing Packages
- Report within 3 days of expected delivery
- We'll investigate with carrier
- Replacement sent immediately for orders $50+

### Damaged Packages
- Report within 24 hours of delivery
- Photos required for insurance claim
- Immediate replacement sent

### Incorrect Address
- Customer responsible for redelivery fees
- New shipping charges may apply

## Holiday Shipping
**Peak Season (Nov 15 - Jan 15):**
- Extended processing times (2-3 days)
- Expedited shipping recommended
- Cutoff dates posted on website

**Holiday Guarantees:**
- Express shipping: Guaranteed delivery
- Expedited shipping: Best effort
- Standard shipping: No guarantee

## Business Delivery
- **Office Buildings**: Lobby delivery standard
- **Signature Required**: Available upon request
- **Bulk Orders**: Special handling available

## Weather Delays
- Carrier delays beyond our control
- Tracking updates provided
- No additional charges for delays

## Subscription Shipping
- **Frequency**: Weekly, bi-weekly, monthly
- **Consistency**: Same day each cycle
- **Modifications**: Update anytime before processing
- **Skipping**: Available in account settings
