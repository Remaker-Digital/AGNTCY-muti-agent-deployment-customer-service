# Coffee Brewing Guides

## Basic Brewing Principles

### The Golden Ratio
- **Standard**: 1:15 to 1:17 (coffee to water)
- **Strong**: 1:14 ratio
- **Mild**: 1:18 ratio
- **Example**: 30g coffee to 450g water (1:15)

### Water Quality
- **Temperature**: 195°F - 205°F (90°C - 96°C)
- **Quality**: Filtered water recommended
- **Mineral content**: 150-300 TDS ideal
- **pH**: 6.5 - 7.5 for best extraction

### Grind Size Guide
- **Extra Coarse**: Cold brew, cowboy coffee
- **Coarse**: French press, percolator
- **Medium-Coarse**: Chemex, clever dripper
- **Medium**: Drip coffee makers, pour-over
- **Medium-Fine**: V60, Kalita Wave
- **Fine**: Espresso, Moka pot
- **Extra Fine**: Turkish coffee

## Pour-Over Methods

### V60 Technique
**Equipment**: Hario V60, filters, gooseneck kettle
**Ratio**: 1:15 (30g coffee, 450g water)
**Grind**: Medium-fine
**Time**: 3-4 minutes

**Steps**:
1. Heat water to 200°F
2. Rinse filter and preheat V60
3. Add coffee, create small well in center
4. Bloom: 60g water, wait 30 seconds
5. Pour in slow, circular motions
6. Finish pouring by 2:30, drain by 4:00

### Chemex Method
**Equipment**: Chemex, Chemex filters
**Ratio**: 1:16 (40g coffee, 640g water)
**Grind**: Medium-coarse
**Time**: 4-5 minutes

**Steps**:
1. Fold filter, rinse with hot water
2. Add coffee, create small crater
3. Bloom: 80g water, wait 45 seconds
4. Pour in 3-4 stages, maintaining water level
5. Total brew time: 4-5 minutes

## Immersion Methods

### French Press
**Ratio**: 1:15 (30g coffee, 450g water)
**Grind**: Coarse
**Time**: 4 minutes

**Steps**:
1. Heat water to 200°F
2. Add coarse coffee to press
3. Pour all water, stir gently
4. Place lid, don't press yet
5. Wait 4 minutes, press slowly
6. Serve immediately

### AeroPress
**Ratio**: 1:14 (18g coffee, 250g water)
**Grind**: Medium-fine
**Time**: 1-2 minutes

**Standard Method**:
1. Insert filter, rinse with hot water
2. Add coffee, level grounds
3. Pour water to fill chamber
4. Stir 10 seconds, insert plunger
5. Press slowly over 30 seconds

## Espresso Brewing

### Basic Parameters
- **Dose**: 18-20g coffee
- **Yield**: 36-40g espresso (1:2 ratio)
- **Time**: 25-30 seconds
- **Grind**: Fine
- **Pressure**: 9 bars

### Dialing In
1. **Too fast/sour**: Grind finer
2. **Too slow/bitter**: Grind coarser
3. **Uneven extraction**: Check distribution
4. **Weak**: Increase dose or decrease yield

## Cold Brew Methods

### Immersion Cold Brew
**Ratio**: 1:8 (125g coffee, 1L water)
**Grind**: Extra coarse
**Time**: 12-24 hours

**Steps**:
1. Combine coffee and room temperature water
2. Stir to ensure saturation
3. Steep 12-24 hours at room temperature
4. Filter through fine mesh or paper
5. Dilute concentrate 1:1 with water/milk

### Japanese Iced Coffee
**Ratio**: 1:15 total (30g coffee, 200g hot water, 250g ice)
**Grind**: Medium-fine
**Method**: Pour-over directly onto ice

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Sour Coffee
- **Cause**: Under-extraction
- **Solutions**: Grind finer, increase water temperature, extend brew time

### Bitter Coffee
- **Cause**: Over-extraction
- **Solutions**: Grind coarser, decrease water temperature, reduce brew time

### Weak Coffee
- **Cause**: Low coffee-to-water ratio
- **Solutions**: Use more coffee, grind finer, ensure proper saturation

### Uneven Extraction
- **Cause**: Poor water distribution
- **Solutions**: Improve pouring technique, check grind consistency

## Coffee Storage Tips

### Whole Bean Storage
- **Container**: Airtight, opaque
- **Location**: Cool, dry place
- **Avoid**: Refrigerator, freezer, direct sunlight
- **Timeline**: Use within 2-4 weeks of roast date

### Ground Coffee
- **Use immediately**: Grind just before brewing
- **If pre-ground**: Use within 1 week
- **Storage**: Same as whole bean

## Equipment Maintenance

### Daily Cleaning
- **Rinse** all equipment with hot water
- **Dry thoroughly** to prevent mold
- **Empty** water reservoirs

### Weekly Deep Clean
- **Descale** equipment as needed
- **Replace** water filters
- **Clean** grinder burrs

### Monthly Maintenance
- **Deep clean** grinder
- **Replace** worn parts
- **Calibrate** equipment
