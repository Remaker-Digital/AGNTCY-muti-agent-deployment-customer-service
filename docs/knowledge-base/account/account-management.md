# Account Management Guide

## Creating Your Account

### Sign Up Process
1. **Visit**: Account creation page
2. **Provide**: Email address and password
3. **Verify**: Check email for confirmation link
4. **Complete**: Basic profile information
5. **Start**: Shopping or set up subscription

### Account Benefits
- **Order tracking**: Real-time shipment updates
- **Purchase history**: Easy reordering
- **Subscription management**: Modify anytime
- **Exclusive offers**: Member-only discounts
- **Faster checkout**: Saved payment methods

## Managing Account Information

### Personal Information
**Editable Fields**:
- Name (first and last)
- Email address
- Phone number
- Communication preferences

**How to Update**:
1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to "Account Settings"
3. Edit desired fields
4. Save changes

### Address Management
**Multiple Addresses**:
- **Default shipping**: Primary delivery address
- **Billing address**: Payment method address
- **Gift addresses**: For gift deliveries
- **Work address**: Office deliveries

**Address Validation**:
- Automatic verification during entry
- Suggestions for corrections
- Confirmation for unusual addresses

## Password Management

### Password Requirements
- **Minimum**: 8 characters
- **Include**: Letters, numbers, special characters
- **Avoid**: Common passwords, personal information
- **Update**: Every 6 months recommended

### Password Reset Process
1. **Click**: "Forgot Password" on login page
2. **Enter**: Email address
3. **Check**: Email for reset link
4. **Create**: New secure password
5. **Confirm**: Login with new password

**Automated Process**: Usually completes within 5 minutes

### Two-Factor Authentication (Optional)
- **Enhanced security** for your account
- **SMS or app-based** verification
- **Enable** in account security settings

### Accepted Payment Types
- **Credit cards**: Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- **Digital wallets**: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Gift cards**: Store credit and gift certificates
- **Buy now, pay later**: Klarna, Afterpay (orders $50+)

### Managing Payment Methods
- **Add new**: Multiple cards for different purposes
- **Set default**: Primary payment method
- **Update expiration**: Automatic renewal reminders
- **Remove old**: Delete unused payment methods

### Payment Security
- **Encryption**: All transactions secured
- **PCI compliance**: Industry-standard protection
- **No storage**: Card details not stored on our servers
- **Fraud protection**: Automatic monitoring

## Account Security

### Security Best Practices
- **Unique password**: Don't reuse passwords
- **Regular updates**: Change password periodically
- **Secure connection**: Always use HTTPS
- **Log out**: On shared computers

### Suspicious Activity
**Warning Signs**:
- Unrecognized orders
- Changed account information
- Unexpected emails

**Immediate Actions**:
1. Change password immediately
2. Contact customer service
3. Review recent account activity
4. Update payment methods if needed

## Account Deletion

### Before You Delete
- **Download**: Order history and receipts
- **Cancel**: Active subscriptions
- **Use**: Remaining store credit or gift cards
- **Consider**: Account suspension instead

### Deletion Process
1. **Contact**: Customer service team
2. **Verify**: Identity and account ownership
3. **Confirm**: Deletion request
4. **Processing**: 7-10 business days

**Note**: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
