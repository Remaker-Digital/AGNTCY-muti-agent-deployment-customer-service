## Account Questions

### How do I create an account?

Step-by-step instructions work well for process questions:

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Verify your email by clicking the link we send
4. Complete your profile with shipping address

### How do I reset my password?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your inbox for the reset link
5. Create a new password (minimum 8 characters)

### How do I update my shipping address?

Navigate to Account Settings > Addresses to add, edit, or remove
shipping addresses. Your default address will be used for all new orders.

## Order Questions

### How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information.
You can also:

- Log into your account and view order status
- Use the tracking number on the carrier's website
- Contact support with your order number

### Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that,
we begin processing and cannot make changes.

To request a change:
- Email: orders@yourcompany.com
- Include your order number and requested change
- We'll confirm within 2 hours if the change is possible

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Apple Pay / Google Pay
- Gift cards

## Product Questions

### What is your warranty policy?

All products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty covering defects
in materials and workmanship. This does not cover:

- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from misuse
- Cosmetic damage

### Are your products [specific attribute]?

Provide the specific information customers are looking for. Be precise
and include any relevant certifications.

Example: Yes, all our coffee is certified organic and fair trade.
Look for the USDA Organic and Fair Trade USA seals on our packaging.
