# Your Policy Title

## Overview

Provide a brief 2-3 sentence summary of the policy. This section should answer
"What is this policy about?" at a high level.

Example: We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all purchases. If you're
not completely satisfied with your order, you can return it for a full refund
or exchange.

## Eligibility

Clearly state who or what this policy applies to:

- Who is eligible (all customers, members only, etc.)
- What products/services are covered
- What conditions must be met

Example:
- All purchases made within the last 30 days
- Items must be unused and in original packaging
- Proof of purchase required

## Process

Provide step-by-step instructions for customers:

1. **Step 1:** Describe the first action (e.g., Contact customer support)
2. **Step 2:** Describe the next action (e.g., Receive confirmation)
3. **Step 3:** Continue with remaining steps
4. **Step 4:** Final step and expected outcome

Include contact methods:
- Email: support@yourcompany.com
- Phone: 1-800-XXX-XXXX
- Chat: Available during business hours

## Timeframes

Specify important deadlines and processing times:

| Action | Timeframe |
|--------|-----------|
| Return window | 30 days from delivery |
| Processing time | 5-7 business days |
| Refund to account | 3-5 business days |

## Exceptions

List any exceptions or exclusions clearly:

- **Not eligible:** Opened consumables, personalized items, gift cards
- **Partial refund:** Items with visible wear (50% refund)
- **Exchange only:** Sale items, clearance merchandise

## Quick Answer

**Most Common Question?**
Direct answer in 1-2 sentences. This is used for quick responses.

Example:
**Can I return opened items?**
No, opened consumable products cannot be returned for hygiene reasons.
Unopened items can be returned within 30 days for a full refund.

## Contact

For questions about this policy:

- **Email:** support@yourcompany.com
- **Phone:** 1-800-XXX-XXXX (Mon-Fri 9am-5pm EST)
- **Live Chat:** Available on our website during business hours
- **Help Center:** help.yourcompany.com
