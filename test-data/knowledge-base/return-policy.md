# Return & Refund Policy

**Last Updated**: January 2026
**Effective Date**: All orders placed after January 1, 2026

---

## Return Window

We accept returns within **30 days of delivery** for most products. To be eligible for a return:

- Products must be **unopened** and in **original packaging**
- Products must be in **resalable condition**
- You must have **proof of purchase** (order number or receipt)

---

## How to Start a Return

1. **Contact us** with your order number and reason for return
2. We'll provide a **Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number**
3. **Pack items securely** in original packaging
4. **Attach the prepaid return label** we provide
5. **Drop off** at any USPS location

**Processing Time**: Refunds are issued within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.

---

## Return Shipping

### Orders Under $50
- **Free return shipping** via prepaid USPS label
- Label provided via email with RMA number
- Tracking included for your protection

### Orders Over $50
- Returns require **support team approval**
- Contact support for return authorization
- Prepaid label provided after approval

---

## Refund Method

Refunds are issued to your **original payment method**:

- **Credit/Debit Card**: 5-7 business days
- **PayPal**: 3-5 business days
- **Store Credit**: Immediate (optional, 10% bonus credit)

---

## Products Not Eligible for Return

The following items **cannot be returned**:

- **Opened consumable products** (soaps, lotions, ingestibles)
- **Final sale items** (marked as "Final Sale" at checkout)
- **Gift cards** and **digital products**
- **Custom or personalized items**
- **Items damaged due to misuse**

**Exception**: Defective or damaged products are always eligible for return or replacement, regardless of condition.

---

## Exchanges

We currently **do not offer direct exchanges**. To exchange an item:

1. **Return the original item** following the process above
2. **Place a new order** for the desired item
3. Consider using **store credit** (10% bonus) for faster processing

---

## Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a **damaged or defective product**:

1. **Contact us immediately** (within 7 days of delivery)
2. Provide **photos of the damage** or defect
3. We'll arrange **immediate replacement** or full refund
4. **No need to return** damaged items in most cases

**Priority Handling**: Damaged/defective claims are processed within 24 hours.

---

## Late or Missing Deliveries

If your order shows **delivered but you didn't receive it**:

1. **Check with neighbors** and building management
2. **Verify the delivery address** in your order confirmation
3. **Wait 24 hours** (sometimes marked delivered early)
4. **Contact us** if still missing after 24 hours

We'll work with the carrier to locate your package or issue a replacement.

---

**Currently, we only ship within the United States.** All return policies apply to US domestic orders only.

## FAQ

**Q: Can I return a product if I opened it to smell it?**
A: Yes, as long as the product seal is intact and it's in resalable condition.

**Q: What if I lost my order number?**
A: We can look up your order using your email address and name.

**Q: Can I return a subscription order?**
A: Yes, individual subscription shipments can be returned following the same policy. To cancel your subscription, please visit your account settings.

**Q: Do you charge restocking fees?**
A: No, we do not charge restocking fees for standard returns.

**Q: How do I track my return?**
A: Use the tracking number on your prepaid return label. You'll receive a confirmation email when we receive and process your return.
