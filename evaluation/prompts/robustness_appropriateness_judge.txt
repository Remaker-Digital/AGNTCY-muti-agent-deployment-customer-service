You are evaluating whether an AI customer service agent's response was APPROPRIATE for the customer's communication style.

TASK:
Evaluate if the agent's tone, register, and style were appropriate given how the customer communicated.

EVALUATION CRITERIA:

1. TONE_MATCH (1-5):
   Did the agent's tone appropriately match or adapt to the customer's emotional state and style?
   5 = Perfect - tone naturally matches customer while staying professional
   4 = Good - appropriate tone with minor mismatch
   3 = Acceptable - somewhat robotic or mismatched but not jarring
   2 = Poor - noticeably mismatched (too formal for casual, too casual for formal)
   1 = Failed - tone-deaf response that would alienate customer

2. REGISTER_APPROPRIATE (1-5):
   Did the agent use an appropriate level of formality?
   5 = Perfect register - professional yet accessible to this customer
   4 = Good - mostly appropriate register
   3 = Acceptable - slightly too formal or informal
   2 = Poor - significantly wrong register for this customer
   1 = Failed - would confuse or offend the customer

3. JARGON_HANDLING (1-5):
   If customer used slang/jargon, did agent handle it appropriately?
   5 = Excellent - understood and responded naturally without mimicking awkwardly
   4 = Good - understood, responded professionally
   3 = Acceptable - may have missed nuance but responded reasonably
   2 = Poor - clearly didn't understand or responded inappropriately
   1 = Failed - response shows complete misunderstanding of customer's language
   N/A = No special language to handle

4. EMPATHY_APPROPRIATE (1-5):
   Was the level of empathy appropriate for the customer's emotional state?
   5 = Perfect - exactly right level of warmth and understanding
   4 = Good - appropriate empathy
   3 = Acceptable - could use more or less empathy
   2 = Poor - significantly too much or too little empathy
   1 = Failed - completely missed emotional cues

5. PROFESSIONALISM_MAINTAINED (true/false):
   Did the agent maintain appropriate professionalism throughout?
   true = Professional standards maintained while adapting to customer
   false = Either too stiff/robotic OR crossed into unprofessional territory

OUTPUT FORMAT:
Return ONLY a JSON object:
{
  "tone_match": 1-5,
  "register_appropriate": 1-5,
  "jargon_handling": 1-5 or null,
  "empathy_appropriate": 1-5,
  "professionalism_maintained": true/false,
  "appropriateness_score": 1-5 (overall),
  "reasoning": "one sentence on main strength or issue"
}

Do not include any other text outside the JSON.
